Hacksaw Monetization & Fair Play Policy

Transparency is vital to our community. This page outlines our approach to server monetization, which is designed to be fair, accessible, and fully compliant with Bohemia Interactive's rules.

Our Core Principles

  • No Gameplay Advantage: We will never sell items, perks, or services that provide a gameplay advantage. Our monetization is strictly limited to cosmetic items and non-gameplay perks like priority queue access.
  • Accessibility for All: All players, regardless of donation status, have a path to obtain the same cosmetic items through regular gameplay and community participation.
  • Supporting the Server: All funds raised are used to cover the significant costs of maintaining high-performance server hardware, ongoing development, and administration.

Hacksaw (HS) Tokens

HS Tokens are our server's virtual currency, used exclusively for crafting cosmetic and aesthetic items.

  • Purely Cosmetic: HS Tokens have absolutely no effect on gameplay and cannot be used to acquire weapons, gear, or any item that would provide an advantage.
  • Available to Everyone: We ensure all players can earn HS Tokens. They can be obtained in several ways:
    • In-Game Loot: Found by looting cash registers throughout the world.
    • Events & Giveaways: Awarded for winning or participating in server events.
    • Donation Gift: Received as a thank-you gift for voluntary donations to the server.
  • No Donator Vendor: All cosmetic crafting is done via the standard in-game crafting bench, accessible to all players. There are no exclusive vendors.

Custom Skins & Ranked ID Cards

We offer a variety of unique, custom-textured cosmetic skins. We have taken special care to ensure these comply with fair play rules.

  • No Camouflage Advantage: Any skins that use camouflage patterns or colors are available to ALL players through the standard crafting system without restriction.
  • Highly Visible Ranked Skins: Our special "Ranked" skins are deliberately designed with bright, vibrant, and highly visible colors. They offer no tactical or camouflage advantage and are purely for cosmetic distinction.
  • How to Obtain Ranked ID Cards: Access to craft these special ranked skins is unlocked by finding a corresponding "Ranked ID Card". These cards are available to the entire community through multiple paths to ensure fairness and accessibility:
    • In-Game: Winning server-wide competitions and events.
    • In-Game: Weekly community giveaways.
    • In-Game: As rare drops during special server events.
    • Donation Perk: Received as a one-time gift when reaching specific cumulative donation tiers. This provides a direct path for those who wish to support the server financially, while free paths remain available for all players.

Third-Party Mod Permissions

All mods and third-party content used on the Hacksaw server are either created by our own team or are used with the explicit, documented permission of the original creators for use in a monetized environment. We are prepared to provide a complete list of permissions upon request.

A unique DayZ experience focused on fair play, community, and challenging survival. We are committed to a fair-play environment and operate in full compliance with DayZ's monetization rules.
