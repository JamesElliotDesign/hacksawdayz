Hacksaw Server Rules

To ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for all players, please read and adhere to our server rules.

General Rules

  • Owners/Admins reserve the right to remove any players/teams that negatively impact the server.
  • Perma Ban for: Duping, Hacking, Cheating, Racism, Sexism, Homophobia, Hate Speech, Toxicity, Impersonating Staff.
  • English is required in global text/voice chat.
  • No camping or logging at POIs. You must CLAIM all POIs as per Discord rules.
  • Do not loot-cycle the same zone between restarts.
  • You cannot buy/sell/gift crafted items from/to players.
  • Report cheaters via a support ticket. Do not call them out in public chat.
  • Video evidence may be required for any support tickets. Lost items are not refunded without evidence meeting compensation requirements.
  • No looting player bodies in PvE zones.

Base & Vehicle Rules

  • One base per person or team. See Discord for specific building dimension limits.
  • Boosting/Raiding player bases is NOT allowed. Secure all property with a codelock.
  • No damaging player vehicles. Looting/stealing unlocked vehicles outside of safe zones is allowed.
  • All vehicles must be stored in a personal or public garage. Vehicles left out for over 24 hours will be impounded.
  • Only one active vehicle per person on the map at any time.

Safe Zone Rules

  • No stealing, scamming, or griefing in safe zones.
  • No trolling (e.g., force-feeding, megaphone spam) in safe zones.

This is a summary of our main rules. For detailed information, compensation policies, and more, please join our Discord.
