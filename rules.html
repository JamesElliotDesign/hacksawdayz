Hacksaw Server Rules

To ensure a fair and enjoyable experience for all players, please read and follow the current rules below.

General Rules

  • Owners/Admins reserve the right to remove any players/teams that negatively impact the server.
  • Perma Ban: Duping, Hacking, Cheating, Racism, Sexism, Homophobia, Hate Speech, Toxicity, Impersonating Staff.
  • English is required in global text/voice chat.
  • No camping or logging at POI's.
  • You must CLAIM all POI's. See Discord claiming-poi rules.
  • All POI's have turret systems that will ruin vehicles, including helis, within the red circle markers (NVZ).
  • Do not loot-cycle the same zone, within restarts.
  • You cannot buy, sell, or gift crafted items from/to players.
  • You cannot gift or spend dono coins to or for other players.
  • Report cheaters via the ticket system. Do not call them out publicly.
  • Clips may be requested as evidence for any tickets.
  • Lost items will not be refunded without evidence that meets compensation requirements.
  • No streaming other servers or games in VC.
  • Public VC channels are for Hacksaw players only.
  • No looting player bodies in PvE.

Base Rules

  • 1 HQ/base per player or per team.
  • A limit of 10 foundation/floor pieces and 3 floors with 10 floor kits per floor.
  • Players can build inside any structure as long as it's not inside a military zone or POI.
  • A limit of 10 base storage containers, 10 base items (functional items, etc.), and 1 crafting bench per player. Trash cans do not count.
  • Boosting/Raiding (decaying) bases is NOT allowed.
  • Secure bases and storage with a codelock.
  • Base checks will take place. Rule-breaking bases will be resized or relocated without warning.
  • Hunter camps are no-build zones.

Vehicle Rules

  • No damaging player vehicles.
  • Looting or stealing unlocked vehicles is not allowed.
  • All vehicles must be stored in personal or public garages.
  • Vehicles left on the map for more than 24 hours will be taken by repo.
  • Only 1 active vehicle per player on the map at any time.
  • You must hold a license to own, buy, or use helis or exotic vehicles.
  • If 2 or more people in your group possess a heli license, you may also buy a helipad and share ONE heli between you. If left outside of a helipad it will be repo'd.
  • Do not drive or fly into the red circle zones on the map.

Safe Zone Rules

  • No stealing, scamming, or griefing items or vehicles in safe zones.
  • No trolling in safe zones (e.g. VC abuse).
  • Vehicles stored in safe zones will be towed within 1 hour of abandonment.

Raiding Rules

  • Raiding is only allowed at verified POIs through events.
  • Raiding groups are allowed, up to 4 members, but one member must have a license.
  • No raiding player bases. No Boosting!
  • Players cannot give away their bases. They must inform an admin if they are leaving.

Gifting Rules

  • Players can gift items in person ONLY.
  • You cannot gift crafted items or tokens of any type.

Vehicle Compensation

Approved Claims

  • Vehicle stolen from safe zone, unless taken by repo.
  • Vehicle destroyed illegally.
  • Bugged or inaccessible vehicle inventory.
  • Vehicle lost due to cheaters.
  • Vehicle lost on restart or crash.

Denied Claims

  • Vehicle destroyed by AI or by owner mistake.
  • Lost lock code.
  • Vehicle destroyed by turret in NFZ.
  • Vehicle lost due to repo.
  • Stolen unlocked vehicles.
  • Water damage.

Hacksaw Tokens

  • Hacksaw tokens are considered a gift and cannot be replaced if lost.
  • Anyone caught stealing Hacksaw Tokens will be issued a ban.

Item Compensation

Approved Claims

  • Items stolen from safe zone.
  • Not receiving purchased items from trader.
  • Killed by cheater.
  • Body looted by player.
  • Lost items on restart or crash.
  • Crafted/Dono items require crafting evidence plus evidence of a server issue.

Denied Claims

  • Lost items due to despawned tombstones within 45 minutes of death.
  • Ruined items.
  • Items stolen by AI.
  • Items ruined or lost from turret.
  • Crafted items stolen or lost in PvP.
  • Lost items.
  • Items stolen from unlocked storage.
  • Items lost from hover loot.

For the most detailed and up-to-date rule clarifications, please check the Discord.
